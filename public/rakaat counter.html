HOW TO USE TECNO NAMAZ RAKAT COUNTER.
Fajr Prayer :

Press Fajr button, "2" rakat will start flashing. Start Namaz, after two sujud one rakat will be completed, after two more sujud your second rakat will also be completed.
During stay in Tashahhud, Next "2" rakat will start flashing, after salam complete these two rakats in the same way as before. After completing of Namaz you will see "8" sign, that shows your Namaz is completed.
'SWITCH OFF POWER BUTTON'

Zuhr Prayer :

Press Zohr button, "2" rakat will start flashing, start Namaz, after two sujud one rakat will be completed, after two more sujud your second rakat will also be completed. During stay in Tashahhud, next "2" rakat will start flashing, after salam complete these two rakats in same way as before. In Zohr Namaz you will perform 2+2+2+2+4. After completing of you will see "8" sign, that shows Your Narnaz completed.
'SWITCH OFF POWER BUTTON'

Asr Prayer :

Press Asr button, ",2" rakat will start flashing, start Namaz, after two sujud one rakat will be completed, after two more sujud your second rakat will also be completed. During stay in Tashahhud, next "2" rakat will start flashing, after salam complete these two rakats in the same way as before.
In Asr Namaz you will perform 2+2+2+2+4. After completing of Namaz you will see "8" sign, that shows your Namaz is completed.
'SWITCH OFF POWER BUTTON'

Maghrib Prayer :

Press Maghrib button, "3" rakat will start flashing, start Namaz, after two sujud one rakat will be completed, after two more sujud your second rakat will be completed, complete the third rakat in the same way as earlier. During stay in Tashahhud, next "2" rakat will start flashing, after salam complete these two rakats in the same way as before. In Maghrib Namaz you will perform 3+2+2 After completing of Namaz you will see "8" sign, that shows your Namaz is completed.
'SWITCH OFF POWER BUTTON'

Isha Prayer :

Press Isha button, "4" rakat will start flashing, start Namaz, after two sujud one rakat will be completed, after two more sujud your second rakat will be completed, complete the third and fourth rakats in the same way as earlier. During stay in Tashahhud, next "2" rakat will start flashing, after salam complete these two rakats in the same way as before. In Isha Namaz you will perform 4+2+2+2+2+2+2+1. After completing of Namaz you will see "8" sign, that shows your Namaz is completed.
'POWER OFF'

WAY TO PERFORM SHORT NAMAZ (FARZ) only

*For Two Farz* Press Fajar button, Two rakat will start flashing, start Namaz, & complete it as normally.

*For Three Farz* press Maghrib button, Three rakat will start flashing start Namaz & complete it as normally.

*For Four Farz* press Isha button, Four rakat will start flashing start Namaz, & complete it as normally.

'SWITCH OFF POWER BUTTON ON COMPLETING EVERY NAMAZ'
